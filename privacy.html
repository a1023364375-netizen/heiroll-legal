HEIROLL

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

HEIROLL is an internal content automation tool used by our food brand to generate and upload short food marketing videos to our authorized TikTok account.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how HEIROLL handles information in connection with its internal content automation tool and TikTok integration. The tool is used for HEIROLL business operations and is not intended as a public service for unrelated third-party users.

2. TikTok API Usage

We use the TikTok Content Posting API with the video.upload scope to upload HEIROLL short videos as draft/inbox uploads. The authorized TikTok user reviews and completes the final posting step inside TikTok.

TikTok authorization data is used only for uploading HEIROLL videos to the authorized TikTok account. We do not use the integration to post on behalf of unrelated third-party users.

3. Information We May Process

The tool may process limited information needed to operate the HEIROLL content workflow, including:

  • HEIROLL video files and related metadata prepared for upload.
  • TikTok authorization information, such as access tokens or refresh tokens.
  • Basic upload status, API response data, and operational logs needed to troubleshoot uploads.
  • Internal business contact information for authorized HEIROLL personnel.

4. How We Use Information

We use information only for legitimate HEIROLL business purposes, including:

  • Generating, preparing, and uploading HEIROLL short food marketing videos.
  • Uploading draft/inbox videos to the authorized HEIROLL TikTok account.
  • Maintaining security, diagnosing technical issues, and improving internal workflows.
  • Complying with applicable laws, TikTok policies, and developer requirements.

5. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell user data. We do not share TikTok authorization data with unrelated third parties for advertising, resale, or unrelated platform use. Information may be shared only when necessary for service operation, security, legal compliance, or with service providers acting on HEIROLL's instructions.

6. Token Security

Access tokens should be stored securely and not exposed publicly. HEIROLL takes reasonable steps to protect authorization data, including limiting access to authorized personnel and avoiding public exposure of credentials, tokens, or secrets in code repositories, client-side pages, or public logs.

7. Data Retention

HEIROLL retains operational information only for as long as needed for the internal content workflow, troubleshooting, compliance, and legitimate business purposes. Authorization data may be removed or refreshed when no longer needed or when access is revoked.

8. User Control and Revocation

The authorized TikTok user may revoke TikTok access through TikTok account settings or other TikTok-provided authorization controls. After revocation, HEIROLL will no longer be able to use the revoked authorization to upload videos through the TikTok API.

9. Children's Privacy

The HEIROLL tool is for internal business use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this tool.

10. Changes to This Policy

HEIROLL may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
